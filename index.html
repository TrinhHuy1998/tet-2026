Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 16, 2026 | Last Updated: February 16, 2026

Welcome to TemGPS. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application TemGPS (the "App"), which provides navigation, messaging, and voice call services.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Name and display name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Profile photo (if provided)
  • Account credentials

1.2 Location Data

To provide navigation and direction services, the App collects and processes your location data, including:

  • Precise location (GPS): Real-time geographic coordinates to provide turn-by-turn navigation, route calculation, and location-based services.
  • Background location: With your permission, the App may access your location in the background to provide continuous navigation guidance.
Important: Location data is essential for the core navigation functionality of TemGPS. You can disable location access at any time through your device settings, but this may limit the App's functionality.

1.3 Communication Data

When you use the messaging and voice call features:

  • Messages: Text messages sent and received within the App.
  • Voice calls: Call metadata (duration, timestamp, participants). We do not record or store voice call audio content.
  • Contacts: With your permission, we may access your device contacts to help you connect with other users.

1.4 Device and Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use the App:

  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App usage statistics and crash reports
  • IP address and network information
  • Language and timezone settings

1.5 Permissions Required

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • Location (Foreground & Background): For navigation and direction services.
  • Microphone: For voice call functionality.
  • Camera: For profile photo capture (optional).
  • Contacts: To find and connect with other TemGPS users (optional).
  • Notifications: To deliver messages, call alerts, and navigation updates.
  • Internet Access: For map data, messaging, and voice calls.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain navigation, turn-by-turn directions, and route planning services
  • Enable messaging and voice communication between users
  • Create and manage your user account
  • Improve, personalize, and optimize the App experience
  • Send push notifications for messages, calls, and navigation alerts
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our services

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With other users: Your display name, profile photo, and online status may be visible to other users for messaging and calling purposes.
  • Service providers: We may share data with trusted third-party services that assist in operating the App (e.g., map data providers, cloud hosting, analytics).
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of TemGPS, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law:

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active and for up to 30 days after account deletion.
  • Messages: Stored on our servers and retained in accordance with our data retention schedule.
  • Location data: Real-time location data is processed temporarily and is not stored long-term.
  • Usage logs: Retained for up to 12 months for analytics and troubleshooting.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data and account.
  • Withdraw consent: Revoke previously given consent for data processing.
  • Data portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out: Disable non-essential permissions through your device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section below.

7. Children's Privacy

TemGPS is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers promptly.
